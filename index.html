The Golden Route to the Bar

A step-by-step interactive guide for Bangladeshi students.

Foundation

Completion of HSC (Bangladeshi Board) or A-Levels with strong academic standing.

01

HSC / A-Levels

Start with your local qualifications or British curriculum equivalents.

02

CertHE Common Law

Entry-level qualification for University of London (UoL) for those coming from HSC background. Typically 1 year.

LLB (Hons) Degree

Complete your 3-year UK LLB through University of London or University of Hertfordshire tuition centers in Dhaka.

03
04

BTC & Call to Bar

Go to the UK for the Bar Transfer Course (BTC/BPC), join an Inn of Court, and finally be called to the Bar.
