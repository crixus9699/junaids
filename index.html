Select your current educational status to see your roadmap.

Route: CertHE Common Law

As an HSC graduate, you cannot enter the 1st year of LLB directly. You must start with the Certificate of Higher Education.

  • Duration: 1 Year (CertHE) + 2 Years (LLB)
  • Min GPA: 3.5 in HSC
  • IELTS: 6.0 recommended

Next Steps:

  1. Enroll in a Dhaka Tuition Center
  2. Register with University of London
  3. Pass 4 modules (Criminal, Public, Contract, Legal System)
  4. Transfer to LLB Year 2

Route: Direct LLB Entry

You are eligible for direct admission to the University of London or Hertfordshire LLB (Hons) program.

  • Duration: 3 Years (Direct LLB)
  • Requirements: 2 Full A-Levels (Min. C, C)
  • O-Levels: Math and English (Min. C)

Direct Entry Benefits:

  1. No Foundation year needed
  2. Immediate access to Year 1 modules
  3. Standard 3-year track to graduation
  4. Lower overall cost than the CertHE route
